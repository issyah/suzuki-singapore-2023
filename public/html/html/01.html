The science of engine design

Thanks to acquired skills from GSX-R1000, the best performing engine is completed. The best sport riding can be experienced and enjoyed.

The liquid-cooled, four-valve, bucket-tappet double-overhead-camshaft (DOHC) engine contributes to class-leading power-to-weight ratio in 150cm3 sport bike class. This achieves superb acceleration and the maximum speed, and improves the pleasure of the best speed feel.

The entire engine speed range from low to high rpms is also an attractive feature of GSX-R150.
